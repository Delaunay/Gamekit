Detailed Description

Quest Types #. Kill #. Kill the right type of enemies

#. Protect Target #. Kill enemies without you or the target dying Variation of the same thing:

#. Escort: protect moving target #. Rescue: i.e Defense & Escort #. Defense: protect static target

#. Delivery Receive Item & deliver it to the target

#. Gather #. Gather Items #. Gather Evidence

#. Combo (requires the player to do something specifc) #. State machine that tracks the player moves

#. Stealth #. Do not get detected

#. Timed #. Finish all the objective on time

#. Race #. Cross a finish line first (might have sub goals to avoid players from making their own track)

Other Features: #. Quest Chains #. Quest Path Guide to the overall location of the quest objective #. Quest Area Highlight the overall area of the quest
